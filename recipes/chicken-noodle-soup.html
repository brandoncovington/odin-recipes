Chicken Noodle Soup

How to Make Chicken Noodle Soup

You'll find the full recipe below, but here are a few tips to keep in mind while you make this classic chicken noodle soup recipe:

Egg noodles are the traditional choice for chicken noodle soup. Rotini and fusilli would work well too, but you can use whatever you have on hand in a pinch.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 4 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can vegatable broth
  • 1/2 pound chopped cooked chicken
  • 1 1/2 cups egg noodles
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and celery and cook until tender, about 5 minutes
  2. Add chicken broth, vegetable broth, chicken, egg noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, slat, and pepper. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
