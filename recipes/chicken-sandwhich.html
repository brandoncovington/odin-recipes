Chicken Sandwhich

Chick-Fil-A Crispy Chicken Sandwhich Copycat

This Chick-Fil-A crispy chicken sandwhich with homemade Chick-Fil-A sauce is the perfect copycat recipe of the classic sandwhich we all love!

We love a spicy chicken at our house! If youd like to add a little heat to your chicken sandwhich, add 1-2 teaspoons of cayenne pepper to the flour mixture when making the breading. I like to coat my kids sandwiches in the flour first, then add the cayenne for spice, and make mine and my husbands.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breast halves
  • 1/2 cup of pickle juice
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • oil for frying
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Pickle, lettuce, tomato and cheese slices, for topping

    • For the Chick-fil-A-sauce

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon kijon mustard
  • 3 teaspoons yellow mustard
  • 2 teaspoon barbecue sauce (hickory tastes the best)
  • 2 Tablespoons honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

    • For the breading:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 Tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1-2 teaspoons cayenne pepper *optional, for a spicy chicken sandwich
  1. Marinate the chicken: combine the pickle juice and water in a ziplock bag. Add the chicken breast halves and marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Make the sauce:Make the Chick-fil-A sacue by combining all ingredients in a bowl. Mix well and set aside
  3. next, in a large bowl mi the breading ingredients together: flour, powdered sugar, black pepper, chili powder, salt, and baking powder.
  4. In another bowl mix the milk, and egg.
  5. Add 2-3 cups of oil to a large saucepan and heat oil to about 350 degrees F.
  6. Coat the chicken:Dip the marinated chicken into the egg mixture, and then coat in th flour breading mixture. Now "double-dip" by repeating this step and dipping the same chicken tender back into the egg mixture and then back into the flour again!
  7. Pan fry:Place chicken in hot oil and gry for 3-4 minutes on each side. remove to paper towel to dry.
  8. Assemble Sandwich:Toast the sandwich buns. Grab the Chick-fil-A sauce and smooth it on both sides of th buns. Top with lettuce, cheese, and crispy chicken, Enjoy!
